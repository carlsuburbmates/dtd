## Recommendations with rationale

1. **Keep Stripe Billing support/reply contact pinned to `info@dogtrainersdirectory.com.au`.**  
Rationale: Stripe invoice emails are generated by Stripe, not by Resend/backend payloads. Keeping this pinned prevents silent drift into a personal mailbox.

2. **Re-run one controlled Stripe invoice send after any Stripe account-profile change and verify both preview + raw source.**  
Rationale: UI preview can hide edge header behavior; dual verification catches display-layer and actual MIME mismatches.

3. **Keep the new email header tests in CI as a non-regression gate.**  
Rationale: `RESEND_FROM`/`RESEND_REPLY_TO` regressions are common during env refactors; CI must fail fast before deploy.

4. **Retain DMARC policy at quarantine while monitoring aggregate reports at `info@`.**  
Rationale: quarantine gives anti-spoofing protection without hard reject risk while sender behavior is monitored post-cutover.
